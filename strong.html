How Strong a player is CompuChess?

CompuChess is able to play at a Master level - (about 2100 ELO), but it can also play at a beginner level for learners and any difficulty in-between. The performance of CompuChess may also vary quite a lot depending on your system.

The main method for changing the level at which CompuChess plays is to bring up the 'Search limits' dialog box- this can be done through the 'Options | computer play options...' menu selection. This dialog box allows you to control how long CompuChess will think about it's move for- making it easier or harder to beat.
