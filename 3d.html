The 3D board menu

Configure 3D board
Configure 3d board dialog

Disable 3D view (Ctrl + D)
Toggles whether or not the 3D board is shown.


Reset camera position
Resets the camera's postion in the 3D view.


Enlarge 3D view (Ctrl + E)
Makes the 3D view fill the entire screen, using this view you can play an entire game just using the 3D representation of the board, it's very much like playing a real game.


Rotate 3D board Left / Right
This rotates the 3D view of the board around by a small amount, the alternative is to right-click in the 3D view and drag left or right to rotate the board.


Zoom In / Out (+/-)
Zooms into or out of the 3D view, this can be useful if you want to look closer at the 3D board.
