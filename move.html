How do I make a move?

When you start CompuChess you will be playing a new game as the white player and it will be your move. There are two ways of making moves in CompuChess, you can either move a piece on the 3D board or or the 2D board.

To make a move on the 3D board simply click on the piece you wish to move with the left mouse button and drag it to it's new position, then release the button. Moving the mouse up and down makes the 3D piece move fowards and backwards on the board.

To make on move on the 2D board click on the image of the piece you want to move with the left mouse button and drag it to it's destination square, then let go of the button. If the move was legal then the piece should appear in it's new position.

To castle, simply move your King to it's destination square using either the 2D or 3D control method, the Rook will automatically move to the other side of the King. Do not move your Rook first as this will just be interpreted as a Rook move- not castling.
