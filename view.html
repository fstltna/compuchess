CompuChess - Online help

How can I change my view of the Board?

CompuChess starts off with two views of the chess board - a 2D view on the right and a 3D view on the left of the screen.

To change the 2D view you can rotate the board 180 degrees by pressing Ctrl + R, or go to:
'Display | 2D board | Rotate 2D board' on the menu. Note that the board is automatically rotated when you play as Black.

To change the 3D view you can rotate the board on it's y-axis by clicking on the 3D viewport with your right mouse button and dragging to the left or to the right. This view can be reset by going to:
'Display | 3D board | Reset camera position'

The 3D view can be disabled through 'Display | 3D board | Disable 3D view' or by pressing Ctrl + D. This may be recommended on low-end systems as it will make the program run more smoothly.

To make the 3D View fill the entire screen, press Ctrl+E or go to 'Display | 3D board | Enlarge 3D view', Beware however that this might degrade performance on a low-end system.
